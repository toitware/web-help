floitsch 12/20/2022 02:22 PM
Jaguar and the portal are two different Toit versions and don't work together.
The older, v1, version is used by the portal, whereas Jaguar uses Toit v2. We generally recommend to stick with Jaguar nowadays, as the code is significantly more up to date.
floitsch 12/20/2022 02:22 PM
That said: if you want to experiment with the portal, then that's still possible.
floitsch 12/20/2022 02:26 PM
Normally, provisioning via Chrome should just work. I googled your issue, but haven't really found a good answer yet.
That said: you can also provision your device with the command-line tool. It's a bit hidden nowadays, but you can download it here: https://archive.toit.io/cli/latest/darwin
